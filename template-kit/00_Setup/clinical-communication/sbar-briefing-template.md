# SBAR Patient Briefing — One-Page Clinical Handover
> **What this is:** A single page you carry to every appointment. Gives any new clinician the essential picture of your case in under 2 minutes of reading.
> **SBAR stands for:** Situation, Background, Assessment, Recommendation — a clinical communication format widely used in hospital handovers, adapted here for patient-held use.
> **How to use:** Fill in once, update whenever your situation changes significantly. Print or show on your phone. Hand it to the clinician at the start of any appointment with someone new to your case.
> **Keep it to one page.** If it runs longer, you have included too much. The full detail lives in your wiki — this is the entry point, not the repository.
